What can I do about sensitive teeth?

Sensitivity can be caused by various factors such as tooth decay, gum recession, or enamel erosion. Your dentist can assess the cause and recommend treatments like desensitizing toothpaste, fluoride treatments, or dental procedures to address the underlying issue.
